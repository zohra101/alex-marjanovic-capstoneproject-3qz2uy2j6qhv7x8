Welcome

Technical communication services are vital for ensuring that complex technical information is accessible to both technical and non-technical audiences, thereby improving user satisfaction and reducing the need for support.


How I can help

I'm a specialist at translating technology: creating and delivering information concisely, clearly, and effectively. Bridging the gap between end users and technical experts, primarily I create help files, design user guides, and write manuals that explain how to use products or services. In addition to writing the content, I also carefully select or create visual aids (charts, diagrams, screenshots, etc.) that help readers understand the information presented.

Services

Whether it's writing user manuals, creating process documentation, or developing training materials, I ensure content is accessible and tailored to your audience's needs.

  • Content Development: Developing content for websites, intranets, and knowledge bases, ensuring that information is accurate, accessible, and user-friendly.
  • Editing and Proofreading: Reviewing technical documents for clarity, consistency, grammar, and accuracy, ensuring they meet organizational standards and guidelines.
  • Information Design: Structuring and organizing information to make it easy to understand and navigate, often involving the use of charts, diagrams, mock-ups, screenshots, wire frames and other visual aids.
  • Technical Marketing: Producing technical marketing materials such as case studies, technical brochures, and product specifications that help explain the benefits and features of technical products to potential customers.
  • Technical Writing: Creating user manuals, installation guides, help files, white papers, and other documentation that explains how to use products or services.
  • User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) Design: Working with design and development teams to create interfaces that are intuitive and easy to use, and conducting usability testing to improve user experiences.
